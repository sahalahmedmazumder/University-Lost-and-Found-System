# Information Gathering

Requirement collection was performed through the following methods.

**Observation**

Observed how students usually report lost items.

***Findings:***
Reports are scattered.
Recovery process is inconsistent.

**Online Research**

Reviewed similar systems and workflows.

***Findings:***
Search and filtering are important.
Dashboard improves administration.

**Discussion**
Collected opinions from potential users.

***Findings:***
Students prefer simple reporting.
Recovery tracking is useful.

**Requirement Summary**

***Required Features:***
***Authentication***
***Item Reporting***
***Search***
***Dashboard***
***Record Management***
