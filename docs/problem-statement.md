#Problem Statement#

Students frequently lose personal belongings inside university premises, including ID cards, wallets, keys, books, electronic devices, and accessories.

Currently, lost and found activities are mostly handled through:

Social media posts
Informal communication
Student groups
Manual announcements

These approaches create several problems:

**Existing Challenges**

Lost items are difficult to track.
Information becomes scattered across multiple channels.
Students often do not know where to report missing items.
Found items may never reach the actual owner.
Manual record management is inefficient.

**Proposed Solution** 

Develop a centralized web application where students can:

Report lost items
Publish found items
Search available records
Track recovery status

Administrators can manage reports and maintain records through a dashboard.

**Expected Outcome**

The system will improve item recovery efficiency, reduce communication barriers, and create an organized reporting process.
